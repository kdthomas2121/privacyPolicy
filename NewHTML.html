MoneyNomad Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy Last updated: 16/12/2022

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install and use the MoneyNomad app ("App").

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you use the App, we do not collect any personal information about you. The App only accesses your device's location in order to determine the currency of the country you are in. This location data is not stored on our servers and is only used for the purpose of converting currencies within the App. The app also records your device ID in order to facilitate MoneyNomad Pro Subscriptions SHARING OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION Your device ID is shared with our subscription service provider, in order to allow the restoration of premium after reinstallation

CHANGES

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the App to stay informed about our information practices and the choices available to you.

CONTACT US If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, or to request the deletion of your data, please contact us at moneynomadapp@gmail.com.
